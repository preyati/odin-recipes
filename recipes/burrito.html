Burrito Bowl

Description

Ingredients:

1
  • Tortilla
  • Rice
  • Black beans
  • Veggies
  • Sour Cream
  • Gaucmole
  • Hot Salsa
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Heat the Tortilla for a while on a pan.
  2. Add about 1/4 cup of cooked rice
  3. Then add 1/3 cup of black beans
  4. Add cooked veggies over the beans
  5. Add the shredded cheese on the top on veggies
  6. Drizzle hot sauce over the rice and beans as per your spice level
  7. Spread the Gaucmole
  8. At last wrap the Tortilla
